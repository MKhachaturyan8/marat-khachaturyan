Marat Khachaturyan

Composer · Neoclassical / Cinematic Music

Marat Khachaturyan is a composer creating neoclassical and cinematic music. His work is centered around piano minimalism, evolving orchestration, and emotional atmosphere.

Inspired by visual imagery and narrative, his compositions explore cinematic form without being bound to a specific medium.
